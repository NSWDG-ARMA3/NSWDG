Overview


Preface

NSWDG Arma-3 Aims to be an authentic historical recreation of select components of Naval Special Warfare Development Group plus some associated support elements set in the mid to late GWOT, this is a hardcore mil-sim environment with extensive skills development. Operations will be brutally long and realistic.

Operations/Gameplay

Operations will be full spectrum and persistent meaning we aim to simulate as much of out JSOTF and associated elements as possible frequently operating out of a airbase with a realistic op tempo. heavy Zeus and player opfor/roleplay will be a core component of what we are trying to simulate we are not looking for generic Zeus made missions on deployment instead we want to simulate a JSOTFs whole area of responsibility having it be a living and breathing environment where dynamic gameplay opportunities and realistic tactics shine. almost all of your experience besides the interview will happen within Arma-3 through TeamSpeak and in game slideshows/documentation to the point where our unit discord acts as a scheduling, support, and logistics resources but is not as heavily relied upon as in other units. for those who what an even more authentic experience a operations portal/dashboard terminal is in development for unit members.

Trainings/Schduleing

Training will be extensive in the capability it provides the element however we recognize very long pipelines can be limiting for those with tight schedules as such we try to make it as efficient as possible. That being said we would like to hop on pretty frequently with the goal of 3-4 4 hours operation blocks a week on deployment phases and 3-4 main trainings with time to hop on whenever available during the week in green team and pro dev/work up phases. Should that training tempo be the case, a class of experienced Arma-3 players Can Likely complete green team in as little as a month. The group environment can be best described as focused on authentic operations we will not be doing PT or hosting ceremonies all we ask is a group of people we are dedicated to authentic recreation tactically and historically to a high degree. Dual claning is generally disallowed.

Mod Development

We have and will continue to develop custom gear and weapons to match periods we deploy and train in with exacting detail. Additionally, we have developed custom systems training assets deployments assets and vehicles to enhance the experience and realism that includes QOL, tactical stance, killhouse training systems, navigation equipment, and much more.
